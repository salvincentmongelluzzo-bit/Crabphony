How To Play

Welcome to Crabphony. Your goal is to selectively breed crabs to unlock rare genetic mutations.

1. Buy tank space and import base Red Crabs.
2. Feed your crabs to trigger the breeding cycle.
3. Harvest lower tier crabs to earn points.
4. Upgrade your genetics lab to increase mutation chances.

Carcinize at 100,000 points to reset the timeline with a permanent multiplier.
